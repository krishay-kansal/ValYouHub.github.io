🕰️ The Time Traveler's ₹100

Imagine I give you ₹100 today… or ₹100 three years from now.

₹100 Today
Year 1 → ₹90.9
Year 2 → ₹82.6
Year 3 → ₹75.1
₹100 in 3 Years

As money travels through time, it loses value due to inflation, risk, and opportunity cost. At 10% discount rate, ₹100 in 3 years is worth only ₹75.10 today!

🎮 The "Choose Your ₹100" Game

🧠 Why Money Loses Value Over Time
🎈
Inflation
Like a balloon slowly deflating, ₹100 buys less tomorrow than today
☔
Risk
Future is uncertain - like holding an umbrella in a storm marked "Future"
🛤️
Opportunity Cost
Two diverging roads - invest now vs. wait. Money can earn returns if invested today
This is why we discount the future. Let's see how.
