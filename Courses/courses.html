DCF (Discounted Cash Flow) &
NPV (Net Present Value)

Value businesses by projecting future cash flows and discounting them to present value. DCF & NPV is a cornerstone of professional valuation.

Calculate the internal rate of return and use IRR to evaluate investments and projects with confidence.

Determine the weighted average cost of capital and apply WACC in valuation, budgeting, and financial decision-making.
